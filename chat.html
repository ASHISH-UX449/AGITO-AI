Help Center

Getting Started

How do I start a conversation?

Simply type your message in the input box at the bottom of the chat interface and press Enter or click the send button. AGITO will respond to your queries in real-time.

What can AGITO help me with?

AGITO can assist with a wide range of tasks including answering questions, generating creative content, helping with coding problems, analyzing documents, translating languages, and much more.

Features

How do I use the Tools section?

The Tools section provides specialized AI capabilities. Click on any tool to open its interface where you can input your requirements and get specialized outputs like generated code, images, or document analysis.

What are Spaces?

Spaces allow you to organize your work into different projects or categories. Each Space can contain its own set of conversations, files, and shared resources with team members.

Troubleshooting

AGITO isn't responding

Check your internet connection first. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page. If you continue to experience issues, please contact support.

How do I report a problem?

You can report any issues through the feedback option in Settings or by contacting our support team directly at support@agito.ai.
